Meet the founder

The story behind Stumbl

Chad Richards grew up in Snellville, Georgia, and first imagined Stumbl while taking an entrepreneurship course at Brigham Young University. The inspiration, however, traces back to an unforgettable experience he had years earlier.

The summerbefore his senior year of high school, he went on a class trip to Italy. After playing frisbee on a beach, they returned to a nearby plaza to eat gelato and found a stage where five women were leading children in a dance. Chad decided to jump in. After one song of dancing by himself, all of his classmates joined him and transformed a fun night into an unforgettable memory.

That was the first time Chad stumbled into just how fun and memorable a local event could be. Today, his mission is to help everyone discover those experiences without having to leave them to chance. Stumbl is designed to feel like the local friend who always knows what is happening nearby, whether you are looking for something to do in your own city or planning local adventures for your next vacation.
